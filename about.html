Where Confidence Meets the Water

At Splash, we teach life-saving swim skills in a fun, safe, and encouraging environment—so every child (and adult!) can feel confident in and around water.

Our Mission

We exist to make water safety accessible and enjoyable for every family. Our curriculum blends proven teaching progressions with positive coaching, so students advance at a comfortable pace while building a love for the water.

Our Story

Splash started as a small neighborhood lesson program and has grown into a community of certified instructors, warm-water pools, and supportive families. We believe strong fundamentals and great experiences create confident swimmers for life.

What Makes Splash Different

Safety First

Small class sizes, constant supervision, and safety skills in every level.

Warm-Water Pools

Heated pools designed to help swimmers relax, focus, and have fun.

Certified Instructors

Friendly coaches trained in our step-by-step curriculum and child psychology.

Family Friendly

Transparent progress tracking and flexible make-ups to fit busy schedules.

Our Curriculum

We teach clear progressions—water comfort, breath control, propulsion, and stroke fundamentals—so each skill builds on the last.

Water Safety

Back floats, safe entries/exits, roll-to-breath.

Beginner

Face in water, kick & glide, assisted streamline.

Intermediate

Unassisted floats, side breathing, freestyle basics.

Advanced

Refined freestyle/backstroke, intro to breast & fly.
